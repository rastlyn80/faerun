Static Housing Documentation

For the statichousing package in the Distro, by Yukiko.

What happens when a player buys a house?

When a player double-clicks on a For Sale sign she is presented with a gump asking if she wants to buy the house. If she clicks "Yes" the system looks first in her backpack to see if she has the required gold to purchase the house. If not, the system checks her bank chest for the required gold. Assuming she has the gold either in her backpack or in the bank, the system will subtract the gold from the appropriate place and she will take possession of the house. I did not program the system to combine the two sources to see if her total gold, backpack and bank chest, equaled the purchase price of the house.

Once the gold the gold is removed she is given two keys, one is placed in her backpack and one in her bank chest.  This system differs from the World of Dreams static housing system because a deed for the house is also placed in her bank chest. The deed allows her to sell her house, using the secure trading gump, to another player if she so desires.

She or a co-owner must either use the house sign or walk into the house before the decay time expires. The setting RefreshTime in the static housing settings determines the number of real life days are allowed before a house "decays" and is returned to a "For Sale" state.
