Static Housing

For static housing in the Distro, by Yukiko.

About Static Housing:

OVERVIEW:
This package is written in such a way that you can provide unattended purchasing of houses by players. For servers
with limited online staff, unattended purchasing doesn't require players to page a GM and wait around to
buy a house. This requires staff to plot, or "pre-deed" the static houses you want to make available for unattended purchase. Pre-deeding is not required. Players can page a staff member and houses can be deeded on demand if you wish but players appreciate being able to buy a house without staff intervention. For role play shards pre-deeded houses that can be bought without staff intervention are very important because it allows for more immersive game play.
