About the Spawn Systems

OVERVIEW:
There are two (2) systems for spawning non-vendor NPCs, spawn points and the spawn system.

Spawn points are used for spawning NPCs in a small area for places such as a brigand camp or a dragon spawn. You can use multiple spawn points for spawning slightly larger areas such as a grave yard or an orc fort.

The spawn system, or region spawner, is better suited to spawning large areas such as a forest or desert area with NPCs randomly chosen from NPC groups. The region spawner can also be used to spawn items or reagents.
