<BR>
<B>Section 1: Definitions</B><BR>
<BR>
<U>1.1 Player</U><BR>
A person who has access to an Ultime Online account and using it to play a character shall henceforth be known as a player.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.2 Staff</U><BR>
Volunteers who include Counselors, Seers, Game Masters, Administrators and Developers easily identified by their job title in front of their name. Such volunteers shall henceforth be known as staff.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.3 Harassment</U><BR>
Abuse, threatening or otherwise violating the legal rights (such as rights of privacy) of others. Publishing, posting, uploading, distributing or disseminating any inappropriate, profane, defamatory, infringing, obcsene, indecent or unlawful topic, name, material or information shall henceforth be known as harassment.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.4 Non Player Character (NPC)</U><BR>
A character generated that is run on scripted AI by the server and not actively controlled by a player shall henceforth be known as an NPC.
<BR><BR>
<U>1.5 Player Killer (PK)</U><BR>
The attacking of another player, willing or not, shall henceforth be known as PK.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.6 Res-Killing</U><BR>
The death of another player multiple times within a short period of time by another player(s), willing or not shall henceforth be known as res-killing.<BR>

<B>Section 2: General Rules</B><BR>
<BR>
<U>2.1 Harassment</U><BR>
Harassment is strictly prohibited. Violating this rule may result in permanent termination of your account.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.2 Paging</U><BR>
Staff are available to aid you with any problems or concerns you may have. Do not page with threatening pages, profanity or obscene and graphic language.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.3 Bugs</U><BR>
Cheating or deliberately using game bugs to disrupt the game is considered a violation of the rules. If you learn of a bug, notify the staff immediately.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.4 Banned Accounts</U><BR>
Do not allow players who have been banned from <THIS SHARD NAME HERE> to access your account. Such a violation will result in the permanent termination of the account.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.5 3rd Party Tools</U><BR>
The use of 3rd party tools is allowed with some caveats. 3rd party tools are to be used solely for the intention of making game play a little less tedious and thus easier.<BR>
- Using 3rd party tools to loot in any fashion is expressly forbidden.<BR>
- Using 3rd party tools to gain an advantage via seeing hidden players or items is expressly forbidden.<BR>
- Using 3rd party tools to modify the look & feel of the game world, such as removing the darkness that comes with in-game nights is expressly forbidden<BR>

Staff members will not and cannot replace lost items, skills or anything else lost due to anything but a bug.<BR>

<B>Section 5: Chain of Punishment</B><BR>
<BR>
<U>5.1 Minor Offenses</U><BR>
You will be notified of what you have done wrong and/or disconnected from <THIS SHARD NAME HERE> and issued a warning that shall be stored in your account file. Repeat offenses will get you jailed for a period of time between one and four days and shall receive another warning stored in your account file.<BR>
<BR>
<U>5.2 Medium Offenses</U><BR>
You will be jailed for a period of time between one and four days and shall receive a warning stored into your account file. Repeat offenses will get you jailed for a period of time between four and nine days and shall receive another warning stored into your account file.
<BR><BR>
<U>5.3 Serious Offenses</U><BR>
Particularly serious violations can result in extended jail time or permanent termination of your account, without warning.<BR>
